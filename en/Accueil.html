That  should I do if my water heater trips my electrical panel?

The resistance of the water heater can end up being damaged and cause the current to cut out. To check it, unplug the device from its socket then disconnect the resistor connectors, isolating them with electrician's tape. We can diagnose and replace the defective part.

​​
How unclog a drain?

First of all it will be necessary to make a test with a suction cup pump, if that is not enough it will be necessary to let act an unblocking product and to retry the maneuver. If your toilet, siphon, shower or washbasin is still clogged, you should contact our company so that a sewer cleaner truck can unclog and clean up your pipe. This will guarantee the proper functioning of your water drains.
